About Us

Established in 2014 Laptop Planet is a specialist retailer catering for the needs of the technological enthusiast, be it a student, office employee or manager.

Our philosophy has always been to stick to what we know and to do it the very best we can.

We offer a wide range of products from top laptop brands to tablets and laptop accesories.

Experience

We know your needs possibly better than anyone else. We can draw upon our vast knowledge to answer almost any questions you may have regarding laptops and their accesories. 

We aim to dispatch all orders within 24 hours, ( the same day if possible if ordered before 3pm ).
